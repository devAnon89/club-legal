Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

This application ("Content Engine") is a private tool used by our team to organise our own car/automotive media archive and publish our own short-form videos to our own social media accounts (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube). It is not offered to the public and does not collect data from third parties.

What we access

  • Our own social accounts. With your explicit one-time authorisation (OAuth), the app receives a token allowing it to (a) publish videos we create to the authorising account, and (b) read performance metrics (views, likes, comments, etc.) for those posts.
  • Our own media. Videos and photos shot by our team, stored on our own server.

What we store

  • OAuth access/refresh tokens, encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM), used only to publish and read analytics for the authorising account.
  • Post identifiers, captions, and the public performance metrics returned by each platform.

We do not sell data, share it with third parties, or use it for advertising. We use no third-party social-media aggregators — the app talks directly to each platform's official API.

Retention & deletion

Tokens are deleted when an account is disconnected. To revoke access at any time, remove the app from your account's connected-apps settings on the respective platform, or contact us to delete the stored token.

Contact

For any privacy request, contact the account administrator at the address provided to the platform during app registration.
